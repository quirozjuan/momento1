One year ago today, I started my addiction to alcohol. I was relying on it for everything. I would down a bottle of vodka to go to sleep. It got to the point where I took a swig of vodka before work. It was spiraling out of my control. This is the song that gave me hope and pushed me to not give up on my dreams and drop the bad habits that I had. I thought about suicide constantly when I drank. I don’t think I would be alive today if I didn’t find this song. This song gave me the strength to start a new chapter of my life and for that I’m forever thankful.
